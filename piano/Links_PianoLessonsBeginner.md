# Beginner piano lessons and tips

Below you will find lessons and information for playing chords and popular music (without the need to read sheet music).
You will most likely still need to read simple *lyric and chord* notation. You can find links to
various piano chord charts [here](Links_PianoChordCharts.md) when you need them.

## Accompaniment patterns

1. [Creative Piano Academy - Josef Sykora | 5 Right Hand Patterns That Turn Beginners Into Pros (video) | 2022](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w3UZHMlWF7U)
   - The introductory text has a link to the sheet music for each of the 5 patterns
   - 1st: The Broken Chord
     * Pattern: Bottom-middle-top-middle
     * Used in Someone Like You by Adele
     * Right hand chord progression: C G Am F
       + C chord:  C E G E | C E G E
       + G chord:  B D G D | B D G D (first inversion)
       + Am chord: A C E C | A C E C
       + F chord:  A C F C | A C F C (first inversion)
   - 2nd: The Classical
     * Pattern: Bottom-top-middle-top
     * Right hand chord progression: C G/C F/C G/C C
       + C chord:  C G E G | C G E G
       + G chord:  B G D G | B G D G (first inversion)
       + F chord:  A F C F | A F C F (first inversion)
       + G chord:  B G D G | B G D G (first inversion)
   - 3rd: The John Lennon
     * Used in Imagine by John Lennon and The Last Resort by the Eagles
     * Pattern: Middle and top together, followed by bottom
     * *Spend the most time on this pattern*
     * Right hand chord progression: F-2nd-inversion C G-1st-inversion Am
   - 4th: The Half-Half
     * Pattern: Middle-bottom - middle-bottom - middle-bottom - middle-bottom (do not play the top note at all)
     * Often played fairly fast with the sustain pedal
     * Right hand chord progression: C Em G F
   - 5th: The All-Rounder
     * Pattern:
       + Beat 1: All 3 notes
       + Beat 2: Rest
       + Beat 3: Middle and top together
       + Beat 4: Bottom
     * Right hand chord progression: F-2nd-inversion C G-1st-inversion Am

1. [Creative Piano Academy - Josef Sykora | 5 Piano Accompaniment Patterns YOU SHOULD KNOW - Piano Lesson (video) | 2022](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rn1ZejMbDR0)

1. [Pianote - Lisa Witt | Left Hand Arpeggio Patterns (Piano Lesson) (video) | 2019](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=q97ZyiBh_pw)
   - Four left hand arpeggio patterns for accompaniment

1. [Pianote - Sangah Noona | Walking Bass line Piano Tutorial (Lesson PDF Included!) (video) | 2022](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Z5_ZQvr1d7w)

1. [Pianote - Lisa Witt | 3 Essential Arpeggios (Beginner Piano Lesson) (video) | 2022](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rmlI_7LgeHM)
   - 1st: 1-3-5-^1-5-3
